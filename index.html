From SaskSuperStars to Rebels

From yellow to black - We are an old club with new name

Rebels Regina Cricket Club was formerly called SaskSuperStars. We played under this name for one season. The name was changed as per the RCA advice on name conflict and new club registration.

Goals and Policy

Club - Goals and Policy

Rebels Cricket Club is entirely run by students of University of Regina. We are deticated in forming a multicultural enviroinment in our team and we welcome any intrested people to join us. As a team we expect individuals and participants to fulfill certain responsibilities and obligations including, but not limited to, complying with Association’s policies, bylaws, rules and regulations, and Statistics of Conduct. Non-compliance may result in sanctions pursuant to this Policy

Rebels Regina Cricket Club objectives and goals

As is stated in Article 2 of the Rebels Regina Cricket Club, the objects of the club shall be to improve, organize, foster, improve, aid and extend the game of cricket.
