Our Mission, Vision & Values

The vision and mission of Rebels Cricket Club are as follows

Reinvent oneself with cricket and Emphasize all could play together irrespective of their ethnicity, social status and background. Binding communities by Empowering Youth to Learn and lead along with Students from University of Regina.

Rules & Policies

League Rules

Rules

Cricket rules during league matches would be strictly followed under Regina Cricket Association’s rules and bylaws . Captains are responsible for ensuring that play is conducted within the spirit of the game during the matches. Players shall not intimidate assault or attempt to intimidate or assault an umpire or a player. It is encourage having a good behavior attitude for maintaining the spirit of cricket.
