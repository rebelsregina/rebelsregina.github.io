Rebels Regina Cricket Club

About

Story of us so Far!!!

Our club was established in 2015 as Sask Super Stars team to play indoor cricket at University of Regina. Then, we played BigBash league and RCA cricket tournament at the city level with a total of 18 games in the summer of 2016. We also had plenty of indoor sessions in the winter of 2016.

Due to some name conflict concerns and as per Regina Cricket Boards’ suggestion, the name was changed to Rebels Cricket Club. So we changed our color too from yellow to black and grey to mark a new beginning.

Entire club activities administered by the University students make the club to stand unique among all other cricket clubs. We look to build a club together with everyone irrespective of their ethnicity, social status and background to play, teach and learn cricket. We believe cricket as a tool to bind people together and bring social changes in the community.

Thanks for reading our story.

Join and play together with us to create the history!!!!!
